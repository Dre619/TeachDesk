# Future Updates

## Student Quick-View Panel
Click any student name anywhere in the app to open a slide-over panel showing their full picture:
- Attendance % and recent absences
- Assessment scores across all subjects
- Behaviour log entries
- Notes

Touches every module (attendance, assessments, behaviour) and makes them feel connected rather than siloed. Highest priority teacher-facing feature.
